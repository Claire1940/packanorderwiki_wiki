| Key / Input | In-Game Action | Practical Warehouse Tip |
|---|---|---|

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Read the Active Ticket">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Collection Route">
    Travel to the designated shelf zones. Use **E** to collect items, and cycle through active orders with **R** to pick up multiple different toys in one sweep.
