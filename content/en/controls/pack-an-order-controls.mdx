Whether you are playing solo or coordinating with a team, your input speed determines your output. Below is a breakdown of the primary inputs you need to internalize for optimal performance.

| Input | Action | Best Practice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E / Left Click** | Pick up or pack items | Use for both shelf collection and box filling. |
| **R** | Cycle active orders | Switch before entering a new warehouse zone. |
| **Q** | Drop held items | Use to clear mistakes near the shelves. |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Cycle carried items | Switch items before reaching the packing station. |
| **Y / Right Click** | Inspect product | Use when items look similar to avoid errors. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always clear your inventory of incorrect items using **Q** right at the shelf where you found them. Carrying them to the packing station wastes precious seconds that could be used for productive tasks.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Warehouse Workflow Optimization">

Efficiency is the key to earning more coins. Your goal is to minimize idle time by grouping tasks. Instead of running back and forth for every single item, plan your route based on the active order ticket.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Review the Ticket">
    Press R to view your current order. Identify the specific items and quantities required before moving away from the packing station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan Your Route">
    Group items by shelf location. If you have multiple orders, prioritize the ones that share common shelf zones to save travel time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect and Verify">
    Pick up required items using E. If you are unsure about an item's identity, use Y to inspect it before adding it to your carry inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pack and Deliver">
    Return to the box, cycle to the correct item using your scroll wheel, and press E to pack. Once the box is full, load it onto the truck.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Boost">
Upgrading your **Carry Capacity** is the most effective way to utilize these controls, as it allows you to complete more of the order in a single trip.
</Callout>
