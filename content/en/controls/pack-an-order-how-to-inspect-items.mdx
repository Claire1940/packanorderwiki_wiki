In the fast-paced warehouse environment of Roblox Pack an Order, accuracy is just as important as speed. When the conveyor belts start moving and several customer tickets pile up, grabbing the wrong product can stall your entire operation. Learning **pack an order how to inspect items** is the single most effective way to prevent packing errors, safeguard your completion times, and secure your place on the global leaderboards.

When similar-looking toys or products are placed close together on the warehouse shelves, a quick verification prevents you from loading incorrect items. The basic workflow requires you to balance observation with physical movement.

| Step | Phase | Core Action | Interaction Input |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Reading | Review active customer ticket | Press R to cycle |

| 4 | Collection | Pick up the correct product | E / Left Click |
| 5 | Packing | Place item in the active order box | E / Left Click |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Spot the Differences">
Always inspect items when dealing with products that share similar shapes or color schemes, such as different flavors of dumplings or similarly colored squishy toys. A split-second check saves you from the time-consuming process of clearing an incorrect box.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Inspecting and Packing">

To maintain a flawless packing streak, you must integrate item verification into your muscle memory. This step-by-step sequence ensures you handle every customer box with professional efficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Cycle and Select Your Target Order">
    Before leaving the packing area, check your active tickets. Press R to cycle through available customer orders. Identify the exact items and quantities required before you make a run to the shelves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate and Inspect the Shelf Items">
    Walk to the designated warehouse shelf. Before picking up the product, hover your reticle over it and use Right Click (PC) or the Y button (Gamepad) to inspect the item. This action displays the exact name of the item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect and Manage Your Inventory">
    Press E or Left Click to collect the verified item. If you have upgraded your Carry Capacity, you can hold multiple items. Use your mouse scroll wheel to cycle through your carried items so the correct toy is active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pack the Box and Load the Truck">
    Return to your packing station, select the correct carried item, and press E or Left Click to place it in the box. Once the checklist on the order ticket is fully complete, carry the finished box to the delivery truck to claim your Coins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Boost">
If you accidentally pick up an incorrect product, do not carry it all the way to the packing station. Press Q immediately to drop the item near its original shelf, keeping your hands and your packing area completely clear of clutter.
</Callout>

</Section>
