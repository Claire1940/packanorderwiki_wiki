<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Farming Coins">

To maximize your earnings, follow this structured farming route. This step-by-step progression is designed to transition you from a slow beginner into a high-speed packing expert.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read and Group Active Orders">
    Before leaving the packing station, check the active customer ticket. Use the **R** key to cycle through available orders. Identify which toys are located in the same warehouse sections so you can collect them together.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute Optimized Shelf Routes">
    Move directly to the target shelves. Collect the required items using **E** or **Left Click**. If you are carrying multiple items, use your mouse **Scroll Wheel** to cycle through them before approaching the packing box.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verify and Pack Accurately">
    Deliver the items to the designated box. If two toys look similar, use **Right Click** or **Y** to inspect them. Packing an incorrect item wastes time because you must press **Q** to drop it and search again.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Load the Truck and Reinvest">
    Once the checklist is complete, pick up the box and load it onto the delivery truck. Take your newly earned Coins straight to the upgrade station to boost your stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Penalty Mistakes">
Placing the wrong item in a box pauses your packing flow. Always inspect items with similar colors or shapes before committing them to the delivery box.
</Callout>
