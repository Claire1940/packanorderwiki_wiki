<Section icon={Package} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Your First 10 Orders">

To afford your **pack an order first upgrades**, you need a clean start. Your first ten orders are the most critical for establishing a "Coin bank." Follow this optimized workflow to maximize your early earnings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Order Selection">
    Enter the warehouse and immediately press **R** to cycle through available orders. Look for orders with 3 or fewer items to complete them quickly and trigger the first Coin reward.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Collection Run">
    Identify the toy locations. Move to the shelves and use **E or Left Click** to pick up the items. If you are unsure of the item, use **Right Click or Y** to inspect it before picking it up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Packing and Verification">
    Bring the items to the packing station. If you picked up an incorrect item, press **Q** to drop it immediately—do not carry it to the box. Pack the correct items and check the ticket checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Truck Loading">
    Once the box is sealed, carry it to the delivery truck. This final step triggers the Coin payout. Repeat this loop until you have enough for your first Speed upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The First Reinvestment">
    Navigate to the upgrade menu. Purchase **Speed Level 1**. You will immediately notice a reduction in travel time, allowing you to complete the next 5 orders 15% faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip: The Scroll Wheel">

</Callout>
