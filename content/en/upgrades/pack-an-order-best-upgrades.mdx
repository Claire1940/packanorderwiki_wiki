<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step progression Route">

To afford the **pack an order best upgrades**, you need a steady stream of Coins. Follow this optimal progression route to maximize your early-game earnings and upgrade your character efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Learn the Warehouse Layout">
    Familiarize yourself with the shelf locations of common toys and food items. Knowing where items like Clickers or Red Squishies reside saves valuable search time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Focus on Order Accuracy">
    Before rushing, use Right Click or the Y button to inspect similar-looking items. Packing the wrong item wastes time because you must press Q to drop it and find a replacement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Your First Speed Upgrades">
    Reinvest your very first Coin rewards directly into Speed. You will notice an immediate difference in how quickly you can complete basic orders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Utility Abilities">
    As you level up, unlock special abilities that complement your core upgrades. Choose abilities that address your slowest tasks, such as searching or packing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Farming Tip">
Use the R key to cycle through active customer orders. Grouping orders that require items from the same warehouse aisle allows you to complete multiple checklists in a single trip.
</Callout>
