To establish a highly efficient routine, follow this step-by-step breakdown of a standard warehouse run. Mastering this loop is essential for completing shipments before the clock runs out.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Game Mode">
    Choose to enter the warehouse alone or join a lobby with friends. Co-op play allows teams to divide shelf sections, while solo play requires a highly optimized individual route.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze the Customer Ticket">
    Look at the active order ticket on your screen. Note the customer name, order number, required items, and exact quantities. For example, Becca might request 1 Red Squishy, 2 Matcha Dumplings, and 3 Salted Butters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate the Shelves and Inspect Items">
    Head into the warehouse aisles. If items look similar in shape or color, hold **Right Click** or press **Y** to inspect them. This ensures you do not grab the wrong toy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect and Manage Your Inventory">
    Press **E** or **Left Click** to pick up the item. If you have upgraded your carrying capacity, use the **Scroll Wheel** to cycle through the items you are currently holding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Pack the Box and Load the Truck">
    Return to the packing station and press **E** to place the correct items into the box. Once the checklist is complete, grab the sealed box and load it onto the delivery truck to earn your Coins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Inventory">
If you accidentally pick up the wrong item, press **Q** to drop it immediately. Do not waste time carrying incorrect products back to the packing station.
