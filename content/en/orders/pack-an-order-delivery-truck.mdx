<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Packing and Loading Guide">

Completing orders quickly requires a systematic approach to shelf navigation and box packing. Follow this step-by-step workflow to streamline your warehouse runs and pack the delivery truck with zero wasted movement.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept and Cycle Your Orders">
    Interact with the active order board. Use the **R** key to cycle through available customer tickets. Analyze the product names, required quantities, and shelf locations before you start moving.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate and Retrieve from Shelves">
    Head directly to the designated aisles. Use **E** or **Left Click** to pick up the requested toys. If you have upgraded your Carry Capacity, collect several different items in one trip.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Inspect and Verify Similar Items">
    If toys look identical or share similar packaging, use **Right Click** or **Y** to inspect them. Confirm the item name matches the ticket checklist exactly to prevent packing errors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pack the Shipping Box">
    Return to your packing station. Use your mouse scroll wheel to cycle through your carried items, then press **E** or **Left Click** to deposit the matching toys into the shipping box.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Load the Delivery Truck">
    Once the shipping box checklist is fully completed, interact with the finished box to lift it. Carry it directly to the rear of the delivery truck and load it to secure your Coins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Packing Penalties">
Placing an incorrect item into a shipping box wastes valuable time. If you accidentally pick up the wrong toy, press **Q** to drop it immediately near its original shelf rather than carrying it to the packing station.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Co-op Roles and Leaderboard Strategies">

Coordinating with teammates is the fastest way to **pack an order delivery truck** and climb the global leaderboards. Without clear communication, players will duplicate work, search for the same items, and leave the loading dock congested.

| Team Size | Recommended Roles | Coordination Strategy | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Solo** | All-in-One | Cycle tickets, batch-collect items, and maximize Carry Capacity. | Backtracking for single items |
| **2 Players** | 1 Picker, 1 Packer/Loader | Picker stays in aisles; Packer manages boxes and loads the truck. | Both players leaving the station |
| **3 Players** | 1 Picker, 1 Packer, 1 Loader | Picker harvests shelves; Packer fills boxes; Loader runs boxes to truck. | Letting completed boxes pile up |
| **4+ Players** | Aisle Specialists, 1 Loader | Assign players to specific shelf zones; Loader coordinates tickets. | Gathering duplicate items |

<Callout type="info" title="Communication is Key">
When playing in multiplayer, use the team chat or voice communication to call out active customer names (e.g., "Gathering Becca's order"). This prevents teammates from wasting time collecting the exact same toys.
</Callout>
