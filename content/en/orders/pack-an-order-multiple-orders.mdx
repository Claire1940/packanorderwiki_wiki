<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Multi-Order Packing Route">

Executing a perfect multi-order run requires a systematic approach. Follow this optimized pipeline to keep your delivery trucks constantly supplied.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read and Cycle Tickets">
    Interact with the active order board. Press **R** to cycle through your available customer tickets. Memorize or note down matching items located in the same warehouse zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan Your Shelf Route">
    Head to the shelf sections containing your target items. Pick up multiple different products in one trip, utilizing your Carry Capacity to hold several toys at once.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Inspect and Verify Items">
    Use **Right Click** (PC) or **Y** (Gamepad) to inspect items that look similar. This prevents packing incorrect products, which resets your progress on that checklist slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cycle and Pack at the Station">
    Return to the packing area. Use your **Scroll Wheel** to cycle through the items in your hands, and press **R** to match the correct active ticket before pressing **E** to pack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Load the Delivery Truck">
    Once all items on a ticket are checked off, interact with the completed box to seal it, carry it to the loading dock, and throw it onto the delivery truck.
  </Step>
</Steps>
