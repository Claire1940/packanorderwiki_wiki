When you first enter the warehouse, your primary focus is reading the active customer ticket. Each ticket displays the customer's name, the order number, a list of requested toys, and the exact quantities needed. Failing to match these details precisely results in failed shipments and wasted time.

| Control Key | Action | Practical Packing Tip |
|---|---|---|
| **E / Left Click** | Pick up or pack item | Check the active ticket before interacting to avoid wrong items. |
| **Q** | Drop held item | Drop incorrect toys near their shelves to keep your hands clear. |
| **R** | Cycle active orders | Cycle through tickets to find items located in your current aisle. |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Cycle held items | Swap to the correct toy in your hand before approaching the box. |
| **Right Click / Y** | Inspect item | Use this to verify similar-looking toys before placing them in boxes. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Out for Similar Items">
Many toys in the warehouse share similar shapes or colors. Always use the **Right Click** or **Y button** inspection feature to verify the exact item name before packing it. Placing a single incorrect item into a box halts your progress.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Order Matching Workflow">

To achieve the fastest times and secure your spot on the leaderboard, you must treat every order like a precision run. Following a structured packing loop minimizes backtracking and eliminates confusion, especially when managing multiple active tickets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read and Cycle Tickets">
    Interact with the active order board. Press **R** to cycle through the available customer tickets. Look for orders that share similar toy types or have items located in the same aisle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan Your Shelf Route">
    Before running into the warehouse, plan your path. Group your targets so you can collect multiple items in a single trip rather than running back and forth for individual toys.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Verify and Grab">
    Locate the items on the shelves. Use the inspection tool to confirm the toy names. Press **E** or **Left Click** to pick them up, and use your scroll wheel to manage your carrying inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Match and Pack">
    Return to the packing station. Scroll to the matching toy in your inventory and press **E** to pack it into the designated box. Repeat this until all checklist items on the ticket are checked off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Load the Truck">
    Once the shipping box is fully packed, pick it up and carry it to the delivery truck. Load it onto the truck to finalize the order, secure your coins, and clear the ticket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Inventory Management Pro-Tip">
If you accidentally pick up an item that does not match your active order, do not carry it back to the packing station. Press **Q** to drop it immediately near its original shelf so you or your teammates can easily find it later.
</Callout>

</Section>
