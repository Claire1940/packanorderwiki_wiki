<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Warehouse Workflow Mastery">

To consistently earn Coins and unlock the **pack an order best abilities**, you must execute the warehouse workflow flawlessly. Follow these steps to optimize your loop from the moment an order appears.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select and Cycle Orders">
    Approach the order board. Press **R** to cycle through active customer tickets. Analyze the list to group items located in similar warehouse zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan and Retrieve">
    Navigate to the shelves. Use **E or Left Click** to pick up the required items. If items look similar, use **Right Click or Y** to inspect and verify their names.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Pack the Box">
    Return to the packing station. Cycle to the correct item using your scroll wheel and press **E or Left Click** to pack it. Drop incorrect items nearby with **Q**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Load and Reinvest">
    Carry the completed, sealed box to the delivery truck. Deposit it to secure your Coins, then head straight to the upgrade shop to boost your stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Verification Tip">
Mispacking a single item ruins your completion time. Double-check item names like 'Matcha Dumplings' vs 'Salted Butter' using the inspection tool before placing them in the box.
</Callout>

</Section>
