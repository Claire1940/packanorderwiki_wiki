<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering Warehouse Controls for Efficiency">

Abilities and upgrades are only as good as the player controlling them. Mastering the PC or mobile controls is vital for ensuring that your increased Speed or Reach doesn't result in accidental item drops or incorrect packing.

| Control Input | Action Performed | Professional Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E / Left Click** | Pick up or Pack item | Tap rapidly when near the box to minimize delay |
| **Q** | Drop held item | Use this to clear "trash" items near the shelf |
| **R** | Cycle active orders | Always check the next order while walking to the truck |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Cycle held inventory | Match the item to the ticket before reaching the box |
| **Right Click / Y** | Inspect item | Use this for similar-looking toys to avoid penalties |

<Callout type="info" title="Inspection Mechanic">
Don't ignore the **Inspect** feature. Some toys, like different colored squishies or similar-shaped boxes, look nearly identical. A 1-second inspection is faster than the 5-second penalty for packing the wrong item.
</Callout>

</Section>
