<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Pack an Order Packing Speed Ability">

To truly master the **pack an order packing speed ability**, you must invest your earned Coins into the core upgrade system. There are three primary stats that determine your effectiveness in the warehouse. While it may be tempting to balance them equally, the most efficient players focus on specific paths depending on their playstyle.

**Speed** is the most universal stat. It reduces the time spent walking between the order station and the shelves. **Carry Capacity** allows you to grab multiple items from the same aisle, which is a massive time-saver for large orders. Finally, **Reach** allows you to interact with items from a distance, meaning you don't have to walk all the way up to a shelf to grab a product.

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Warning">
Do not ignore Carry Capacity in solo play. While Speed is flashy, being forced to walk back and forth for every single item will significantly lower your overall orders-per-minute.
</Callout>

| Upgrade Type | Core Function | Solo Priority | Team Priority |

By focusing on Speed first, you ensure that every other action in the game happens faster. Once you reach a comfortable movement level, shifting focus to Carry Capacity (for solo) or Reach (for teams) will provide the best return on investment.
