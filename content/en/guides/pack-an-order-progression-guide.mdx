<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Stages">

Progression in **pack an order** is divided into distinct phases. As you earn more Coins, you unlock abilities that further streamline the process. Following a structured path ensures you don't waste resources on sub-optimal upgrades early on.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Rookie Phase (Levels 1-5)">
    Focus on learning the shelf layout. Don't worry about speed yet; focus on **accuracy**. Every perfect order gives a small bonus that adds up. Save all your initial Coins for your first two Speed upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Efficiency Phase (Levels 6-15)">
    Start using the **R key** to manage multiple orders. This is where you should invest heavily in **Carry Capacity**. Being able to hold 3-4 items allows you to clear the "Toy" and "Food" sections in single sweeps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ability Unlocking (Levels 16-30)">
    Unlock your first active abilities. These tools are designed to reduce the friction of finding products or packing boxes. Check the Abilities menu regularly and choose the one that addresses your biggest bottleneck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Specialist Phase (Levels 31+)">
    By now, you should have high Speed and Carry Capacity. Focus on **Reach** to shave off those final seconds. This is the stage where you start competing for the global leaderboards by optimizing your pathing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

During the mid-to-late game, the "Farming Loop" becomes vital. This involves picking a specific set of orders that are physically close to each other in the warehouse and ignoring outliers until your movement speed is high enough to compensate for the distance.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid the 'Clutter' Trap">
Many players drop unwanted items right in the middle of the aisle. This creates physical obstacles that can slow down your movement. Always drop items **under** the shelves or in designated trash areas to keep your paths clear.
</Callout>
