<Section icon={Target} title="Essential Controls and Navigation">

Speed is nothing without control. Utilizing the correct keybinds allows you to cycle through active orders, drop incorrect items instantly, and manage a stacked inventory on the fly. Memorizing these inputs makes your movement much more fluid.

| Input | Action | Practical Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **E / Left Click** | Pick up or pack item | Interact with shelves, boxes, and delivery trucks |
| **Q** | Drop currently held item | Toss incorrect items near their shelves to clear space |
| **R** | Cycle customer orders | Check alternative orders to match nearby shelf items |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Cycle carried items | Switch to the exact product needed before packing |
| **Right Click / Y** | Inspect item | Verify names of similar-looking toys to avoid mistakes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Packing Penalties">
Placing the wrong item in a box wastes valuable time. If you accidentally grab an incorrect toy, press **Q** to drop it immediately near its home shelf rather than carrying it to the packing station.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Solo Speedrun Strategy">

For players looking to dominate the solo leaderboards, executing a highly optimized route is mandatory. Follow these steps to maximize your solo performance and earn coins quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Ticket">
    As soon as the round begins, press **R** to review your active orders. Identify the items that are clustered closest together in the warehouse.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Batch Collect Shelves">
    Run to the furthest required shelf first. Grab all requested items in that section using your Carry Capacity before working your way back toward the packing station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Double-Check Similar Items">
    Use **Right Click / Y** to inspect toys that look identical. Verifying the exact item name before packing prevents costly mistakes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sequence the Packing">
    Approach the order box, use your **Scroll Wheel** to cycle to the correct item, and press **E** to pack. Repeat rapidly until the box is full.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Deliver and Reinvest">
    Load the completed box onto the truck. Head straight to the upgrade shop to spend your Coins on Speed to make your next run even faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Callout>
