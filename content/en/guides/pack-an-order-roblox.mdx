<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Upgrades and Progression Path">

To keep up with increasingly demanding order sizes, players must reinvest their hard-earned Coins into core character upgrades. The game features three primary upgrades: **Speed**, **Carry Capacity**, and **Reach**. Balancing these stats is the key to climbing the leaderboards.

| Upgrade | Primary Effect | Best Suited For | Solo Priority | Team Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Speed** | Increases movement velocity | Reducing travel time between shelves and trucks | **1** | **1** |
| **Carry Capacity** | Increases maximum inventory slots | Transporting multiple different items in one trip | **2** | **3** |
| **Reach** | Extends interaction distance | Rapid shelf collection and quick box packing | **3** | **2** |

The optimal progression route depends heavily on your team size. Solo players face a massive travel bottleneck, making Carry Capacity highly valuable early on. Co-ordinated teams can divide shelf territories, making Reach far more effective for snatching items from adjacent aisles.

<Section icon={Target} title="Controls and Input Guide">

Executing actions quickly requires muscle memory of the game's control layout. Misclicking can cause you to drop items or pack incorrect products, which resets your progress on that specific box checklist.

| Input Key | Device | In-Game Action | Practical Application Tip |
|---|---|---|---|
| **E / Left Click** | PC (Mouse/KB) | Pick up or pack item | Stand close to the shelf or box before pressing |
| **Q** | PC (Keyboard) | Drop held item | Drop wrong items near shelves instead of packing areas |
| **R** | PC (Keyboard) | Cycle active orders | Use this to review other tickets while searching |
| **Scroll Wheel** | PC (Mouse) | Cycle carried items | Align the correct toy in hand before approaching the box |
| **Right Click / Y** | PC / Gamepad | Inspect item | Use this to verify similar-looking toys before packing |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Order">
    Press **R** to view available customer tickets and choose your active target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Retrieve and Inspect">
    Navigate to the shelves, pick up items with **E**, and use **Right Click** to inspect and verify names.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cycle and Pack">
    Use the **Scroll Wheel** to select the correct item from your inventory, then press **E** at the box to pack it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="System Info">
If you accidentally pick up an item that is not on any active order, press **Q** immediately to drop it. Carrying dead weight limits your inventory space and slows down your overall packing rate.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Co-op and Leaderboard Strategies">

Playing with a team changes the gameplay dynamic entirely. Instead of every player running through the entire loop individually, assigning dedicated roles maximizes efficiency and prevents players from duplicating efforts.

| Team Size | Recommended Setup | Key Roles | Common Mistake to Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Solo Run** | All-rounder route | Collector, Packer, Loader | Returning to base with only one item at a time |
| **2-Player Team** | Split duties | 1 Runner (shelves), 1 Packer (loading/boxing) | Both players running to the same aisle |
| **3-Player Team** | Full specialization | 1 Picker (aisles), 1 Packer (boxes), 1 Loader (trucks) | Leaving the packing station completely unattended |
